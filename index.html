» cv Research and bio

My research is centered around the security and privacy of social and information networks, with an emphasis on problems that broadly impact the way people use the Internet.

I received my PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of British Columbia under the mentorship of Konstantin Beznosov and Matei Ripeanu.

I'm currently involved in research projects related to cyber security intelligence (DRDoS), Internet measurement (vulnerability scanning), Android security (crypto misuse), and Tor hidden services (deanonymization).
