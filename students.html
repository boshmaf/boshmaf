Research is Fun!

« Yazan Boshmaf

A very warm welcome to you, dear Student. Thank you for your interest! By choosing to conduct scientific research, you embark on an exiting journey of exploration and discovery. Like all pursuits that matter, it is a long, sometimes frustrating, journey, but one that is generous, humbling, and extremely rewarding.

I have put together a bunch of useful content on this page. I would skim through the links below before exploring mentorship and internship opportunities, which are equally important. Enjoy!
